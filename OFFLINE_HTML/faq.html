1. FAQ


1.1 Is it possible to rate a person?

  • No. Logged user can rate comments (positive or negative) concerning of their usefulness one time.

1.2 How can I rate a comment?

  • You have to click on a comment (or on a pin). Now you can see the comment. At the bottom of the comment field you have the possibillity to rate this comment.

1.3 How will be generate a thread?

  • A thread will be generate automatically. Several comments with the same attributes will be generate to a list and visualized as a list on the left site on the main page.

1.4 How can I change my personal settings?

  • Logged users have the possibillity to change their personal settings over the gear-wheel (on the right side of the logged main page).

1.5 Who is CommenTerra?

  • You can find further informations about CommenTerra when you click on the link at the footer "about us"

1.6 How can I deactivate the user-help?

  • You can deactivate the user-help in your user-settings

1.7 Can I save my search-requests?

  • Yes. You can safe your search-requests by click on the ""-button

1.8 How can I manage my search-requests?

  • By clicking on the gear-wheel you open the personal settings. Here you have the chance to manage (rename) the requests
